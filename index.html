HOW TO PLAY

What is the Drummer Manager Game?

It's a community-driven, live-streaming experience where you help manage the drummer "21" through suggestions and support. Your participation is key to the story!

What are "NUMBERS" and how do I get them?

"NUMBERS" are our loyalty points. You can earn them by watching the stream, participating in chat, and achieving community goals. They are used to influence in-game decisions and objectives.

How can I support the stream?

You can support by donating, subscribing, following on all platforms, and engaging with the community goals. Your contributions directly impact the game's progression!

When will the game end?

“I'm only 32... and a human life is full of surprises. So… not anytime soon.”

What are livestream alerts?

You can trigger sounds or effects live using points (e.g., Twitch, Lumia Stream). Great for fun, chaos, or celebrations!

How do I earn NUMBERS?

Watch, participate, financial support, creativity, or redeem engagement rewards on supported platforms.

Can I really change the game?

Absolutely. The more you interact, engage, and participate through NUMBERS, the more influence you have over the game’s direction and outcome. Quadratic voting helps!
